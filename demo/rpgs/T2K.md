Twilight: 2000 is a tabletop role-playing game set in a near-future post-nuclear war environment focused on survival.

It operates on military skills, resource scarcity, and realistic logistical constraints in a collapsed world.

It produces grounded narratives of small-unit action, survival decisions, and moral ambiguity.

It does not support high-fantasy elements, supernatural systems, or power-escalating character progression.
